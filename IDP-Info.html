Interdisciplinary Project

(IDP)

This project was conducted in 2019 with my teammates .

Throughout this project, we learned the skills crafting sharp and accurate interview question for interviewing,

managing data we received from interviewee , and presenting them using different model

In this project, other than helping on gathering data, I contributed mainly on poster and brochure design.
